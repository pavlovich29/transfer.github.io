We are professionals! Correctness, accuracy, speed and comfort – the main criteria for our proposition of chauffeur service or car rental with driver. Our drivers will do their best to road traffic in the city, traffic and emergency situations did not affect the plans of our clients.

If you need to meet foreign guests or important person, this type of lease will allow you to make a good impression. You can focus on discussing while the driver takes his job.

  •  We provide any kind of road transport for your request. There is a car premium and business-class brands such as Mercedes, AUDI, BMW.
  •  We are pleased to offer you such services as: Minsk Airport transfers, hourly rental, ride from town to town on business activities, excursions or private trips.
  •  Our drivers are carefully selected according to their professional skills, knowledge and attitudes to road work.
  •  Our drivers speak English language.

Chauffeur service – has become one of the most popular services today among many circles of society. As a rule, people who need a car with driver or chauffeur service, book a taxi. However, this is not always convenient. In addition, ordering a taxi, you do not know exactly which model of car you drive up, and if you plan to drive a long time – it can be a very expensive proposition.
That’s why we are ready to provide you the cars with a driver who will take you to the appointed place on time. The cost of such services to fixed – we know in advance how much to pay for a certain period of time.

The main advantage of this service – you do not get distracted on the road, and you know that you can do your business during the way.
